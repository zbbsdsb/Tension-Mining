# Tension Mining Template: Organization Design

> Use this template when designing organizations, teams, or organizational structures.

---

## When to Use This Template

- Designing a new company or team structure
- Reorganizing an existing organization
- Analyzing organizational dysfunction
- Designing organizational culture
- Planning organizational scaling

---

## Pre-Filled Tension Checklist

Identify tensions shaping your organization:

### Core Tensions (select all that apply)

- [ ] **Freedom vs Efficiency** (`T-FRE-006`)
  - How much autonomy do teams have?
  - What processes ensure coordination?
  - When does freedom become chaos?

- [ ] **Innovation vs Stability** (`T-STA-009`)
  - How do you encourage new ideas?
  - What structures maintain operational continuity?
  - How do you prevent innovation from destabilizing operations?

- [ ] **Centralization vs Decentralization** (`T-CEN-011`)
  - What decisions are made centrally vs locally?
  - How does information flow?
  - What are the bottlenecks?

- [ ] **Specialization vs Resilience** (`T-SPR-016`)
  - How specialized are roles?
  - What happens when specialists leave?
  - How do you maintain cross-functional capability?

- [ ] **Short-term vs Long-term** (`T-SHO-019`)
  - What metrics drive behavior?
  - How do you invest in long-term capabilities?
  - What prevents short-termism?

- [ ] **Individual vs Collective** (`T-IND-007`)
  - How are individual contributions recognized?
  - What collective goals bind the organization?
  - How do you prevent free-riding?

## Pre-Filled Invariant Checklist

Identify invariants relevant to your organization:

- [ ] **Identity Drives Cooperation** (`I-IDC-001`)
  - What is the organizational identity?
  - How does culture enable collaboration?
  - What sub-identities exist?

- [ ] **Boundaries Shape Behavior** (`I-BSB-001`)
  - What boundaries define teams and departments?
  - How do boundaries affect communication?
  - What happens at boundary crossings?

- [ ] **Tradeoffs Are Inescapable** (`I-TAE-001`)
  - What tradeoffs has the organization explicitly accepted?
  - What tradeoffs are implicit?
  - How are tradeoffs communicated?

- [ ] **Feedback Loops Stabilize** (`I-FLS-001`)
  - What feedback mechanisms exist?
  - How does the organization learn?
  - What prevents runaway positive feedback?

- [ ] **Local Rules Create Global Order** (`I-LCG-001`)
  - What local rules govern team behavior?
