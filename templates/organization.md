### Domain Analysis Dimension

Organizations are **intentional systems** — they are designed with explicit goals but evolve through human behavior. Unlike biological systems, organizations can change their structure through conscious decisions. When analyzing phenomena, consider both the **intended structure** (org chart) and the **actual structure** (who talks to whom, where decisions really get made).
