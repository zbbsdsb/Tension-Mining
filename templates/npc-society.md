# Tension Mining Template: NPC Society Construction

> Use this template when designing NPC societies, game AI ecosystems, or synthetic social systems.

---

## When to Use This Template

- Designing NPC behavior for open-world games
- Creating emergent narrative systems
- Building synthetic economies
- Designing social simulation games
- Investigating emergent behavior in multi-agent systems

## Pre-Filled Tension Checklist

Identify tensions shaping your NPC society:

### Core Tensions (select all that apply)

- [ ] **Survival vs Exploration** (`T-SUR-014`)
  - How do NPCs balance safety and risk?
  - What happens when survival is too easy or too hard?
  - How does exploration drive narrative?

- [ ] **Individual vs Collective** (`T-IND-007`)
  - How do individual NPC needs conflict with group welfare?
  - What collective structures emerge?
  - How do you prevent tragedy of the commons?

- [ ] **Emergence vs Design** (`T-FRE-006`)
  - How much do you script vs let emerge?
  - What emergent behaviors are desirable?
  - How do you prevent undesirable emergence?

- [ ] **Variety vs Performance** (`T-SPR-016`)
  - How many unique NPC behaviors can you simulate?
  - What is the performance cost of variety?
  - When are identical NPCs acceptable?

- [ ] **Freedom vs Efficiency** (`T-FRE-006`)
  - How much autonomy do NPCs have?
  - What constraints ensure the game remains playable?
  - How do you prevent chaos without over-controlling?

- [ ] **Stability vs Adaptation** (`T-STA-009`)
  - How does the society respond to player actions?
  - What structures persist vs change?
  - How do you prevent society collapse?

## Pre-Filled Invariant Checklist

Identify invariants relevant to your NPC society:

- [ ] **Local Rules Create Global Order** (`I-LCG-001`)
  - What local rules govern NPC behavior?
  - What global structures emerge?
  - How do you tune local rules to produce desired global patterns?

- [ ] **Gradients Drive Movement** (`I-GDM-001`)
  - What gradients exist in your world (wealth, safety, opportunity)?
  - How do NPCs follow or create gradients?
  - What flows emerge from gradient-following?

- [ ] **Identity Drives Cooperation** (`I-IDC-001`)
  - What identities exist in your society (factions, families, professions)?
  - How does identity enable or prevent cooperation?
  - What conflicts arise from identity differences?

- [ ] **Variation Enables Selection** (`I-VES-001`)
  - How much variety exists among NPCs?
  - What selection pressures operate?
  - How does the society evolve over time?

- [ ] **Feedback Loops Stabilize** (`I-FLS-001`)
  - What feedback mechanisms maintain social order?
  - What positive feedback drives change?
  - How do you prevent runaway feedback?
