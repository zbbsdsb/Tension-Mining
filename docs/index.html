The Methodology

Seven Phases. Strict Order. No Skipping.

Each phase has a gate condition. You cannot proceed until it is met. The AI interviews you one question at a time. You drive the content; the methodology enforces the discipline.

Phenomenon Mining

Observe reality before building abstractions. Collect 5-10 real-world examples from at least 3 distinct domains.

Gate: 5+ phenomena from 3+ domains

Tension Mining

Identify forces pulling the system in different directions. Ineliminable tradeoffs, not "pros vs cons."

Gate: 5+ tensions with Force A/B identified

Invariant Mining

Extract principles that appear across multiple tensions and domains. The "laws" of complex systems.

Gate: 3+ cross-domain invariants

Mechanism Mining

Study how reality resolves tensions. Nature, society, and technology have already developed solutions.

Gate: 3+ mechanisms mapped to tensions

System Synthesis

Combine tensions, invariants, and mechanisms into a coherent model with predicted behaviors.

Gate: Coherent model with failure modes

Algorithm Synthesis

Only now design algorithms. Derive them from mechanisms, not from first principles.

Gate: Algorithms mapped to Phase 4 mechanisms

Destruction Phase

Attack the model. Identify weak assumptions, missing tensions, and possible redesigns.

Gate: Failure analysis + D1-D5 evaluation

Install in 30 Seconds

# User-level (all projects)
